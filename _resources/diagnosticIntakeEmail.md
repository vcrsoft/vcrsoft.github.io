Before dispatch, the biggest problem is often not the repair.

It is missing information.

Customers describe problems in different ways, leave out important details, or provide incomplete context — forcing dispatchers and technicians to fill the gaps later.

These examples show what happens when customer intake is captured in a structured format before scheduling.

Each Diagnostic Intake report includes:

* Problem summary  
* Key observations from customer intake  
* Site and contact details  
* Full conversation record for additional context  

The goal is not remote diagnosis.

The goal is better preparation before the first visit.
