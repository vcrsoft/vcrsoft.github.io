## Intake Conversation Log

<pre>
Customer: Our kitchen has completely lost power — nothing is working.
System: Is the circuit breaker for the kitchen tripped?
Customer: No, everything looks normal.
System: Are other parts of the building affected?
Customer: No, dining and office still have power.
System: Have you tried resetting any GFCI outlets?
Customer: Yes, but no change.
System: Was there any burning smell, pop, or equipment failure before this?
Customer: None at all.
System: Understood. Likely issue may be breaker, panel connection, or feed fault.
System: Capturing business and contact details for intake report.
Customer: Joe’s Diner, 3456 Joe Lane, Chicago. Joe White, Manager.
System: Thank you. Preparing structured report for service technician.
</pre>
