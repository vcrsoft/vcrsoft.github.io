## Intake Conversation Log

<pre>
Customer: We have a leak under the restroom sink. Water pools when it is used.
System: Does the leak occur continuously or only during use?
Customer: Mostly when the sink is running, sometimes drips afterward.
System: Have the shut-off valves been inspected?
Customer: Yes, they function but show minor dripping.
System: Any visible pipe damage or cracks in the P-trap?
Customer: No cracks. P-trap looks intact but is damp.
System: Any recent plumbing work in this restroom?
Customer: No recent work.
System: Understood. Likely cause may be a loose fitting, worn washer, or valve seal issue.
System: Capturing business and site details for intake report.
Customer: Sunset Cafe, 120 Main St, Springfield, IL.
System: Contact confirmed: Linda Perez, Operations Manager.
System: Intake complete. Preparing structured report for service team.
