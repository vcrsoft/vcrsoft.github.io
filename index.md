<h3 style="font-size: 1.5em; color: #333; border-bottom: 2px solid #eee; padding-bottom: 0.3em;">
How It Works
</h3>

<p style="line-height: 1.5;">
Each contractor gets a simple branded intake link.  
When a customer reports an issue, the chatbot guides them through structured questions about symptoms, equipment, and conditions.
<br><br>
The result is a <strong>structured diagnostic report sent by email</strong>, including key details, summarized issue context, and contact information — ready for dispatch or technician review.
</p>
